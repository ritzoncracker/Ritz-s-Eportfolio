CIVIL AIR PATROL: Operations Plan for Cadet Staff Selection

During my time as Cadet Commander in my Civil Air Patrol squadron, there was a time where the squadron needed new staff to accommodate growth. The fashion in which the staff selection process went in the past was never formally documented or outlined. So, I took it upon myself to create a formal Operations Plan in accordance with Civil Air Patrol documentation standards. This plan was tailored to the needs of our squadron at the time but even now can be used in special cicumstances such as not having enough cadet offcers to fill flight commander positions, etc.

COVA CCI Cybersecurity Scholars Research Program

In spring of 2023 I participated in the Coastal Virginia Commonwealth Cyber Initiative (COVA-CCI) Undergraduate Research Program that pairs studens with mentors to research and write a final paper on any cybersecurity-related topic of the student's choice. I chose to focus on the legal side of cybersecurity. It is a series of three short essays, overall grouped into one 29-page paper titled A Few Legal and Practical Challenges in the Investigation of Cybercrime
